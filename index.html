DBC SF Lunch Spot Guide

Here is a list of some of my favorite lunch spots so I'm only including restaurants that are within walking distance of our campus. DBC campus is closer to the south side of the financial district which is not as busy but there are still some good eats to be found. Though some restaurants are closed for dinner and weekends.

Restaurant Cuisine Type Location Notes
Little Skillet Soul Food 360 Ritch St. Chicken and Waffles
Garaje Mexican 475 3rd St. Tacos, burritos, beers!
Perilla Vietnamese 510 Mission St. Five spice chicken with garlic noodles
